Moon Rider: Luna
================
<br />

Statistic
--------
- Strength：15 + 1.9
- __Agility__：22 + 2.8
- Intelligence：16 + 1.85
- Attack Animation(攻击抬手): 0.46 / 0.54 
- Damage(初始攻击): 48 - 54 
- Casting Animation(施法抬手): 0.6 / 0.4 
- Armor(初始护甲): 3.08 
- Base Attack Time(基础攻击间隔): 1.7 
- Movespeed(移动速度): 330 
- Missile Speed(弹道速度): 900
- Attack Range(射程): 330 
- Sight Range(视野范围): 1800 / 800
